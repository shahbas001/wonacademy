About Us

The WON Multi-sport Academy is a world class project intended to nurture and support the younger generation with their passion in sports. Our aim is to facilitate international training for kids in various type of sports and to bring out the best of their latent talent inherent in them. They are provided with an atmosphere of innovativeness, competitiveness, and intellectual independence that helps to shape them to achieve greater heights in their professional lives. This will be a unique and first of its kind Academy in India.

We are the pioneers in this field.Our academy will consist of facilities to school children from an early age. We will have full fledge schools for all age groups, additionally we will have graduation and post graduation courses to help the candidate to utilize their full potential.

>We also have sport research center, premium hotels and state of the art hospital facilities.
